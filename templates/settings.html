How do I change my password?

Go to the Account section and fill in the "Change password" field, then click Save Changes.

How do I delete my account?

Click "Delete Account" in the sidebar. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

How do movie recommendations work?

Recommendations are based on your viewing history and the genres you've set in Preferences.
